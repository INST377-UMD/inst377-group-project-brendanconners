About the Project

Thank you for visiting our page about our weather and other natural event warning system for travelers to Asia! We are a team of students at the University of Maryland, College Park, partaking in a group project for our INST377 HTML course. As each member of the group hopes to be able to visit Asia in the future, we figured our project could be done in a way that helps us in the future! That being said, we found a great API from the Hong Kong Observatory, which allows the public to access information regarding potential storms, earthquakes, and Tsunamis. By using this API, we figured that we can make planning to go to Asia a better experience by avoiding potentially dangerous natural events.
